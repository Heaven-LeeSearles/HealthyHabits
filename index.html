About Us

Healthy Habits Incorporated is a non-profit organization that was founded in 2012, by Patricia Williams, an ER nurse who brought together a small team of concerned members of society, like herself, who have witnessed young children develop early onset health issues. Our company strives to bring awareness to communities such as schools and families on how they can build and incorporate healthier habits for both children and adults alike.

Our Objective

From the start, our goal was to help people get well, stay well and make the most of their life. We are dedicated to mobilizing schools, families, and communities to create healthier lifestyles for kids. We educate and create action plans to help reduce outbreak of illnesses and allow people to live healthier and longer. We are passionate about helping others be well
